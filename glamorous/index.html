Sunday April 26, 2009 at Buddies In Bad Times Theatre
12 Alexander Street, Toronto
Doors: 8 p.m.  Performance: 9 p.m.

Swingin’OUT, Toronto's queer swing dance group, presents an evening of glitz, glamour, and entertainment inspired by the 1920's, 30's, and 40's, in support of The 519 Church Street Community Centre. Glamorous: A Tribute to the Jazz Age will be hosted by the lovely Samantha Stone, and will feature dance, drag, and burlesque performances by...

Bobby Pinn
Breakprance Crew
Ceci My Playmate
Mlle. Crème Brûlée
Dick DuBois
Donnarama
PushPULL Dance
Skin Tight Outta Sight
Swingin’OUT
The Saucy Tarts

Dress in your jazz-era finest and stay to dance the night away!

Tickets: $10 in advance, $12 at the door

All proceeds will go to The 519 Capital Campaign, and the first $4000 raised will be matched by an anonymous donor. In addition to the show, there will be a silent auction, and donation forms will be available (donations are eligible for the matching, too).

Advance tickets are now available at The 519 or at Swingin’OUT's weekly dance classes. For ticket information or to make alternate arrangements, please contact glamorous@swinginout.ca.

Put your best foot forward for an interactive evening of dance!

May I Have This Dance?  will provide you with a sample of what Toronto's queer partner and group dance scene has to offer.

Enjoy dance performances and demos. Take mini lessons in salsa, square dancing, merengue, line dancing, and swing. Try out your new moves during the DJed sets of music, and once you've worked up an appetite from all that dancing, check out the bake sale.

Date: Friday, June 13th, 2014
Time: 6:00 pm - 10:30 pm
Location: The 519 Church Street Community Centre, 2nd floor Ballroom
Admission: $7 or pay-what-you-can

Accessibility
The 519 Community Centre is wheelchair-accessible with a ramp at the entrance, an elevator to the second floor ballroom, and a wheelchair-accessible washroom. The 519 Community Centre also has gender-neutral and gender-specific washrooms.

Scent-Free Policy
Please refrain from wearing heavy scents, often found in colognes and perfumes. Because you will be dancing in close proximity to each other, this is an accessibility issue for individuals with multiple chemical sensitivities or who are scent-sensitive.

Safe Dance Space
Our dance clubs strive to provide a welcoming and safe space for all participants. For example, Swingin’OUT follows these guidelines. You have the right to refuse an invitation with anyone and to stop a dance at any time.

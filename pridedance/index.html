Put your best foot forward for an interactive evening of dance!

May I Have This Dance?  will provide you with a sample of what Toronto's queer partner and group dance scene has to offer.

Enjoy dance performances and demos. Take mini lessons in salsa, square dancing, merengue, line dancing, and swing. Try out your new moves during the DJed sets of music, and once you've worked up an appetite from all that dancing, check out the bake sale.

Date: Friday, June 13, 2014
Time: 6:00 pm - 10:30 pm
Location: The 519 Church Street Community Centre, 2nd floor Ballroom
Admission: $7 or pay-what-you-can

This event is presented by LGBT Dance, Triangle Squares, Rainbow Ballroom, Toronto Wranglers, and Swingin'OUT.

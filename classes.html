Fall 2009 classes begin on Thursday, September 17. See our calendar for details.

Classes will be held in the Friendship Room at Saint Luke's United Church, which is located at 353 Sherbourne Street, on the southeast corner of Sherbourne & Carlton. The entrance is on Sherbourne, south of the main church entrance. The Friendship Room is located up the stairs, at the end of the long hallway.

Beginner Class
No experience or partner is necessary for this drop-in class. We review the basics every week.
Time: 7:00 pm
Cost: $5 (sliding scale available)
Social Dancing/Practice
Most classes finish up with a bit of social dancing, so you can practice what you learned. Everyone is welcome!
Intermediate/Advanced Classes
Also held on Thursday evenings, these classes are by invitation only for those students who have mastered the basics.
